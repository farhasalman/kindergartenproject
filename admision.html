Admission Information

We welcome you to Kiddystars, a place where your child can explore, grow, and thrive. Below, you will find all the necessary information about our admission process, important dates, and requirements. We look forward to welcoming your family into our community!

Admission Process

Follow these simple steps to complete your child’s enrollment at Kiddystars:

  • 1. Complete the online admission form available on our website.
  • 2. Schedule a tour of our facilities to meet our teachers and see the learning environment.
  • 3. Submit the required documents: Birth Certificate, Immunization Records, and Parent ID Proof.
  • 4. Pay the admission fee to secure your child’s spot.

Important Dates

Keep these dates in mind for a smooth admission process:

  • Application Start: March 1, 2024
  • Application Deadline: May 31, 2024
  • Admission Results: June 15, 2024
  • School Year Begins: August 1, 2024

Admission Requirements

Please ensure you meet the following criteria for successful admission:

  • Children must be between the ages of 3 to 5 years.
  • Submit all required documents before the deadline.
  • Attend an interview or orientation session with the child.
